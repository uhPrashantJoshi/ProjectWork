ABOUT US

Cty is a website which provides an on-the-go travel companion for people to use as a service,
which helps them in their planning by providing them with information weather in the
cities across the world, plane booking services, as well as hotel booking services.

Our website provides quick and easy access to necessary information, vital to plan an
all-round good experience when it comes to a trip. The user gets to check the weather of any
city in the world, get access to book flight tickets between any two cities, and access to book
hotel rooms in a major part of the world.
